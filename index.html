🔁 Sync from Zentra Stock
✓ Same-Domain Live Sync Active
Both apps are on zentra-suites.vercel.app — they share the same storage automatically. Click Sync Now at any time to pull the latest data from Zentra Stock into your accounting books.
1
Open Zentra Stock & copy sync data
In Zentra Stock, go to Settings → 📙 Zentra Books → Copy Sync Data. One click copies everything to your clipboard.

2
Come back here and click Import
Click Paste & Import below. Zentra Books reads your clipboard and creates all journal entries automatically.
